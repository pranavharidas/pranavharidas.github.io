Research

My research interests broadly lie in Complex Analysis. Currently, I am studying surface braid groups and, more generally, the mapping class group of hyperbolic surfaces with Chitrabhanu Chaudhuri. I am also exploring the complex analytic properties of Teichmüller spaces.

During my postdoctoral stint, I explored proper holomorphic mappings of a certain class of domains in \( \mathbf{C}^n \). In a joint project with Jaikrishnan, we studied proper holomorphic mappings in special classes of domains in SCV.

During my doctoral studies, I studied quadrature domains in higher dimensions and the Green's function on planar domains.
