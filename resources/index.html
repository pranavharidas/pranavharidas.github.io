Dr. Pierre Albin from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign offered a course on Algebraic Topology. The course more or less follows the route taken by Allen Hatcher in his book Algebraic Topology. The lectures can be found here.

Lectures on the Geometric Anatomy of Theoretical Physics

Dr. Frederic P. Schuller delivered a series of lectures titled Lectures on the Geometric Anatomy of Theoretical Physics. The lecture series involves building the foundational mathematics needed for theoretical physics — including Classical Mechanics, Electromagnetism, Quantum Mechanics, Statistical Physics, and Relativity. These lectures begin with Logic and progress toward Bundle Theory, offering an excellent route into the material usually found in a Topology and Geometry course in Mathematics.
